## I. INTRODUCTION
This is the management system for the **Morneven Institute** website, which serves as the community hub for the **Gemora Universe**. Here, users engage in **RP (Roleplay)** as personnel whose primary task is creating content to be integrated into the Gemora Universe.

- **Content:** Can consist of OCs (Original Characters) or illustrations synchronized with the Gemora Universe.
- **Project:** Collaborative content such as illustration collabs or matured concept collabs (characters/creatures/technology/etc) that must strictly align with the Gemora Universe.
- **Lore:** If project content is approved/passed, it will be added to the Lore page.

---

## II. PERSONNEL MANAGEMENT (Personnel Rules)

### 1. Registration & Tracks
* **Registration:** To register a new personnel account, users fill in their email, username, and password. Upon initial registration, they will receive **PL1-GOV (Intern)** status by default.
* **Track Transfer:** If a personnel member wishes to switch tracks (**ENG, OPS, or LOG**), they may apply for a track transfer to the current track's **PL5** personnel via the `Management` menu on the `Transfer` tab by selecting the target track and providing a reason. The person in charge reserves the right to reject the transfer for reasons that are not clear or valid.

---

### 2. Clearance Level (PL) Rules

#### **[ PL1 - Intern ]**
* **Access:** LP1 personnel can only view available content.
* **Upgrade:** If personnel wish to gain upload access to the gallery, they may apply for a clearance upgrade to **PL2** via the `Management` menu on the `Clearance` tab by selecting "Upgrade Clearance" and providing a reason while agreeing to the PL2 terms. Requests will be reviewed by the **PL4** of their current track.

#### **[ PL2 - Active Personnel ]**
* **Obligation:** Once personnel have attained PL2, they are required to contribute content at least **once per month (1/month)**.
* **Upload:** Content can be uploaded via the `Management` page on the `Report Submission` tab. There will be two choices: *Team Project* (PL3 only) and *Personal Project*. Select **Personal Project**.
* **Review & Publication:** Submissions will be reviewed by your track's PL4. **Accepted content will be forwarded/uploaded to the Gallery.** PL4 reserves the right to reject content if it violates institutional regulations with valid evidence.
* **Timing System:** The countdown starts at the beginning of the month and ends at the end of the month.
* **Sanction:** Failure to meet the quota will result in a demotion back to **PL1**.

#### **[ PL3 - Senior Personnel / Lead ]**
* **Requirements:** LP3 can be obtained by applying through the `Management` page under the `Clearance` tab by selecting "Upgrade Clearance to PL3," provided you are a PL2 for at least one month after your upgrade and have registered a team of 2 to 5 members (including the Lead).
* **Member Constraints:** Members must hold PL2 status, and there is a maximum of 2 PL3 members allowed per team.
* **Obligation:** PL3 is required to complete at least **one project per year (1/year)** (not necessarily as the team leader).
* **Team Management:** PL3 may apply to add/remove members via the `Management` page on the `Team` tab by providing a reason and evidence of consent from the added or removed personnel, subject to approval by the track's **PL4**.
* **Project Application:** PL3 can apply for a project by registering it on the `Report Submission / Team Project` page, filling in project goals, reasons, etc. Projects will be reviewed by PL4, and the approving PL4 will become the project supervisor.
* **Privilege:** PL2 members involved in a project are exempt from the one-content-per-month obligation, provided the leader reports monthly progress by updating the *project patch*.
* **Reward:** If the team meets its quota before the year ends, they are exempt from upload/project obligations until the following year.
* **Timing System:** The project countdown starts at the beginning of the year and ends at the end of the year.
* **Sanction:** The team will be disbanded and the Clearance Leader will be demoted to **PL2** if the team fails to meet the quota (failed project or incomplete without reason). Approved projects will appear on the Project page.

#### **[ PL4 - Supervisor / Mentor ]**
* **Requirements:** LP4 is obtained by applying for an upgrade.
* **Obligation:** LP4 is required to supervise **2 projects that reach completion per year**.
* **Upload Privilege:** **PL4 and above can upload to the gallery without a review**, provided the content still meets the criteria.
* **Sanctions & Control:** If content does not comply, a revision will be requested or it will be subject to **forced takedown**. If the annual supervision quota is not met, a sanction in the form of **Access Restraint** (cannot comment/discuss or upload to the Gallery) will be applied for one month; if persistent, they will be demoted to PL3.

#### **[ PL5 - Operational Director ]**
* **Promotion Level:** Promotion to PL5 (Operational Director) is an administrative career leap that cannot be achieved through standard application, but rather through proven dedication and high managerial competence.
* **Key Requirements:**
    1. Served as PL4 for at least 3-6 months.
    2. Earned the *Track Master* title by successfully supervising at least 4 PL3 projects without major issues.
    3. Passed the *Operational Test* by drafting a strategic development plan for their respective track unit (ENG, OPS, or LOG).
* **Procedure:** The application process is conducted formally through the `Executive Promotion` tab and will be screened directly by **PL6** and **PL7 (Author)**. The evaluation phase involves an in-depth review by PL6 regarding how the candidate handles conflict or submission rejections; the final decision rests with the Author's veto power.
* **Core Authorities:**
    - **Conflict Mediator:** Acts as the second-tier judge to mediate disputes between PL4 and PL3 regarding content eligibility.
    - **Resource Management:** Responsible for approving "Official Assets" to ensure they remain consistent with the main lore.
    - **Site Announcement:** Access to post official announcements on the website's *News Feed*.
    - Manages personnel track transfers.

#### **[ PL6 - High Mediator ]**
* **Status:** LP6 are personnel trusted by the Author (**Redacted/(website admin/LP7)/Redacted**) to mediate and manage personnel.
* **Authority:** Holds full authority according to their respective track.
* **Obligation:** LP6 is obligated to create **events** for personnel.
* **Sanction:** Personnel can report LP6 if they abuse their authority; if validated, LP6 will be sanctioned according to the violation, with the most severe being a **Permanent Ban**.

#### **[ PL7 - Author / Admin ]**
* **Identity:** Website Admin / LP7 / Redacted.

## III. CONTENT MANAGEMENT
1. **Gallery Content:** Displays individual work from PL2 (passed review) and direct uploads from PL4 and above (subject to regulatory oversight).
2. **Project Content:** Collaborative content from PL3 teams that appears after approval from the supervisor (PL4). Progress is displayed through monthly patch updates.
3. **Lore Content:** The final archive of the Gemora Universe for project content that has been completed and approved by management (**PL6 & PL7**).
